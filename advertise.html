Questions

Frequently Asked

Is the Basic listing really free?
Yes! Getting listed on PitQuest is completely free. Your restaurant will appear on our app and website with your name, address, phone number and hours at no cost.
How quickly will my restaurant be listed?
Basic listings are typically added within 48 hours. Featured and Premium listings are set up within 24 hours of payment confirmation.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — monthly plans can be cancelled at any time with no penalty. Annual plans are non-refundable but you keep your listing for the full year.
What is a "homepage spotlight"?
Premium members are featured in the "Top Rated BBQ Spots" section on the PitQuest homepage and app — the first thing users see when they open the app.
How do I send my photos?
After signing up, simply email your photos to pitquest1@gmail.com and we'll add them to your listing within 24 hours.
Do you offer catering or food truck listings?
Yes! We have a dedicated Rollin Smoke section for BBQ food trucks. Contact us for catering and food truck advertising options.
